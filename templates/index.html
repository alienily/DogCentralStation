DOG CENTRAL STATION

Follow the links below to view data for each corresponding table in the SQL database:

Appointments
- View upcoming dog grooming appointments. This is created from an intersection table between our Appointments and Employees Entities. This is because there is a M:N relationship here as one dog may require multiple employees to be groomed. Like a giant fluffy Samoyed!
Customers
- List of customer information including name and membership date. Includes Search functionality.
Dogs
- List of dog information used when setting appointments. Includes search functionality and a Boolean caution field for those especially vicious little dogs that like to bite groomers hands!
Employees
- List of staff including names and positions at the company. Includes Search functionality.
Items
- List of current inventory including names, prices and available quantity. Includes Search functionality.
Purchases
- Basically a ledger, a book or balancesheet showing itemized purchases. Each row is drawn from our PurchasesItemized table and contains the purchase ID, item ID, item name and purchase date of each itemized...purchase. Confusing nomenclature here but we settled on using this intersection table as it tracks the quantity of a single item sold through a M:N relationship between our Purchases and Items tables.
